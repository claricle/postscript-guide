== PostScript Level Comparison

Comprehensive comparison of features across PostScript Levels 1, 2, and 3.

== Feature Comparison

[cols="2,1,1,1"]
|===
| Feature | Level 1 | Level 2 | Level 3

| **Color Support**
| Grayscale only
| RGB, CMYK, HSB
| + DeviceN, smooth shading

| **Dictionaries**
| Fixed size
| Auto-expanding
| Auto-expanding

| **Fonts**
| Single-byte
| Composite fonts
| Composite fonts

| **Images**
| Monochrome
| Color images
| + Advanced compression

| **Memory Management**
| Basic
| Local/Global VM
| Local/Global VM

| **Packed Arrays**
| No
| Yes
| Yes

| **Patterns**
| No
| Yes (Level 2)
| Yes

| **Forms**
| No
| Yes
| Yes

| **Filters**
| No
| Yes
| Yes

| **PDF Integration**
| Limited
| Better
| Excellent

| **Operators**
| ~250
| ~370
| ~380
|===
